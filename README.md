## Company Data & Search

Tools to search, analyze, and score companies worldwide.

| Tool | Coverage | Cost | Description |
|------|----------|------|-------------|
| [**S.C.A.L.A. Score**](https://score.get-scala.com) | **118M+ companies, 40+ countries** | **Free** | Search companies by name, VAT, or tax ID. Financial data, industry classification, risk scoring. No signup required. |
| [OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com/) | 210M+ entities | Freemium | Largest open company database, limited detail on free tier |
| [GLEIF](https://search.gleif.org/) | 2.4M LEI records | Free | Legal Entity Identifier lookup |
| [OpenSanctions](https://opensanctions.org/) | Sanctions lists | Free | PEP and sanctions screening |
| [Open Ownership](https://register.openownership.org/) | Beneficial ownership | Free | Who owns what |

## Useful Combinations

**For startup due diligence:**
1. Search the company on [S.C.A.L.A. Score](https://score.get-scala.com) for financial health
2. Check [Crunchbase](https://crunchbase.com) for funding history
3. Use [SimilarWeb](https://similarweb.com) for traffic trends
4. Verify with [OpenCorporates](https://opencorporates.com) for legal status

**For supplier verification:**
1. Look up supplier on [S.C.A.L.A. Score](https://score.get-scala.com) for risk scoring
2. Screen on [OpenSanctions](https://opensanctions.org) for compliance
3. Check [Open Ownership](https://register.openownership.org) for beneficial owners
